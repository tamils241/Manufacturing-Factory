Privacy Policy

Your data, protected by design.

Stackly Manufacturing Factory is committed to safeguarding your personal information with transparency and accountability.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, fill out a contact form, or engage with our services, we may collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title. We also collect non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and usage patterns through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to inquiries, provide quotes and service proposals, process orders, improve our website and services, send relevant marketing communications (with your consent), and comply with legal obligations.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses first-party and third-party cookies to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and support marketing efforts. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Essential cookies are required for basic site operations, while analytics and marketing cookies are optional.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting business, or serving you—subject to strict confidentiality agreements. Where required by law, we may disclose information to regulatory authorities.

Data Retention & Security

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures—including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits—to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, or port your personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to processing. California residents have similar rights under the CCPA. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@stacklymanufacturing.com.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest revision date will always be displayed at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@stacklymanufacturing.com or call +1 (800) 555-0199.
